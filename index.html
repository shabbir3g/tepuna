Custom carved wooden gifts that convey the spirit of New Zealand

Te Puna Design gifts are proudly made in New Zealand using high-quality native timbers and materials.

Te Puna Design makes beautiful and unique New Zealand Wooden Gifts that tell stories. You'll find them in elegant homes and offices all throughout the world.

Using the natural beauty of rimu, swamp kauri and rimu veneer, Te Puna Design crafts elegant Kiwi souvenirs including wooden boxes, coasters, wine glass holders and bookmarks.

Embellished with your choice of striking paua inlays, each design symbolises an important element of New Zealand culture or heritage.

Functional and beautiful, our custom-carved products are a perfect keepsake of your trip to New Zealand, or a way to share your Kiwi experiences with friends and loved ones.

Meet the Craftsman

Te Puna Gifts are crafted in a Tauranga workshop by New Zealander John Evans.

After milling and drying, the timber is cut and engraved using a laser engraving machine. The product is then assembled, sanded and finished with a paua insert. A simple lacquer is applied for a natural finish (this does not affect the natural colouring of the wood).

John also makes the cardboard packaging that the gifts are shipped in, and can supply shelving and display stands for retailers and gift shop owners.
